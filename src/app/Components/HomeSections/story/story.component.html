The Story of T4T

Celes-Té was founded with a vision: to bring the luxury of Artisanal Tea to everyday life by Anubha Jhawar, a certified Tea Sommelier from the UK Tea Academy, London.

The story begins in the heart of India, amidst beautiful tea gardens, where she discovered the art of crafting the perfect tea blend.
